# India Allied Health Professionals Gap Analysis & Strategy Platform

A comprehensive strategic planning dashboard for analyzing and addressing the **6.5 million Allied Health Professional workforce gap** in India to achieve WHO's Universal Health Coverage targets.

## Problem Statement

India faces a critical shortage of Allied Health Professionals (AHPs), with an estimated **95% gap** against WHO benchmarks. This dashboard provides data-driven insights and strategic recommendations for workforce planning and investment decisions.

## Features

- **3D Interactive Visualizations** - Explore AHP gaps across categories
- **10-Year Budget Trend Analysis** - Historical health spending patterns
- **Multi-Scenario Comparison Engine** - Baseline, no-intervention, and proposed strategies
- **Inflation-Adjusted Cost Calculator** - Real-time investment projections
- **Comprehensive Strategy Formulation** - Phase-wise implementation roadmaps
- **Geographic & Demographic Analysis** - State and region-wise breakdowns
- **AI-Powered Policy Recommendations** - Scenario-based insights
